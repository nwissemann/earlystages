companion

A Notion workspace to help answer your “what do I want to do with my life”.

The companion is a free, two-part Notion template that will guide you through two major pillars to 4x your mental clarity.

  • Life Steering → clarify your values, define your North Star, and design your personal trajectory.
  • 180 Questions → build a reflective journaling habit through daily prompts that deepen clarity and focus.

what’s in it for you in life steering:

☑️ Clarify your core values through three lenses

☑️ Identify your North Star, your long-term vision

☑️ Set your goals and design systems to reach them

☑️ 180 guided journalling prompts to spark reflection and focus

☑️ Structured around Mind, Method, and Meaning

☑️ Optional progress & streak tracking

the early stages companion is perfect for you if:

✅ You’re in an early or changing stage of life and want more clarity about what really matters

✅ You love thinking deeply but struggle to turn reflection into action

✅ You’ve tried other Notion setups that felt too complex or too productivity-driven

✅ You want one calm space to align your mind, method, and meaning
