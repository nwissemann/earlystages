what makes a life feel well-lived?

I have no clue, but I’ve been trying to figure it out for the past decade or so. By testing different career paths, learning from much smarter people, and exploring different schools of thought, habits, or (semi-)spiritual practices.

This is a small, non-optimized personal site I vibe-coded during a cold, rainy Berlin winter. Glad you’re here.

Here’s what you will find here

  • about — who I am and how I got here
  • blog — my newsletter on second-order careers
  • resources — books, people, and tools that shaped how I think
  • contact — how to get in touch

Most of my thinking lives in the newsletter, if you want to follow along.
